5. Transport Slice Characteristics "Transport slice realization" is the use of a specific technology to
realize the SLOs in the operator's network. While the transport
slice itself is technology agnostic, its realization is technology
specific. A transport slice may be realized by allocating
appropriate resources into one or more networks. The managing entity
realizing a transport slice is referred to as realizing network
operator in this document.
5.1. Characteristics as Network/Service A transport slice is built with a combination of specific
technologies on the basis of request from a higher operations system.
The interface to higher operations systems should be technology-
agnostic, and slice customers don't need to recognize concrete
configurations based on the technologies. The request to instantiate
a transport slice is represented with some indicators such as SLO,
and technologies are selected and managed accordingly.

4.2.1. Stakeholders
A transport slice and its realization involves the following
stakeholders and it is relevant to define them for consistent
terminology.
Customer or User: A customer is a user of network slice and hence a
user of transport slice. Customers may request for monitoring of
associated resources or specific changes to them.
Transport Slice Operator: A transport slice operator (or slice
operator for short) manages one or more transport slices for one
or more customers.
Transport slice Controller: Using a Transport Slice Controllr (TSC),
the slice operator realizes the transport slice in the netowkr
using various technologies and also maintains the run-time state
of resources and topologies associated with it. The transport
slice controller also monitors the transport slice health (see
section x.x for more details)
Transport Network Operator: A transport network operator (or network
opertor for short) is traditional network infrastructure operator
who offers network resources to slice operator to be used for
realization of a particular transport slice. Network operators
utilizes existing network controllers for specific technology to
realize transport slices in its network.
